Hi, I'm Alex!

I'm a designer, software developer, aspiring PM, language learner, and obsessive traveler, currently completing a dual BS / MS in Computer Science at Stanford University.

I moved to the US from Israel when I was nine and currently live in the Bay Area. This past summer I was an Engineering Project Manager intern at Apple, where I shipped a project to show automated encyclopedia facts for entities looked up on iOS Spotlight.

I am fascinated by human-computer interaction, language learning (am quanti-lingual), entrepreneurship, travel, and design. I have an obsession with user-facing products and am particularly interested in bridging cultures and facilitating travel through technology.
